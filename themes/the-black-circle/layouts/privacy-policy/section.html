Privacy Policy

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

This Privacy Policy explains how The Black Circle Group (“TBC”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, processes, and shares Personal Data in connection with your use of our website and related interactions.

The Black Circle Group operates through affiliated entities across multiple jurisdictions. The entity responsible for processing your Personal Data will depend on the context in which it is collected and will be identified in any relevant contractual documentation where applicable.

For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, including the Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) Data Protection Regulations 2021, an appropriate affiliated entity of The Black Circle Group will act as the data controller depending on the context in which your Personal Data is collected and processed.

ABOUT OUR WEBSITE AND SERVICES

The website at theblackcircle.com is the official website of The Black Circle Group and is intended to provide information about the Group, its affiliated entities, and its offerings.

We may collect and process Personal Data submitted through the website in connection with enquiries, relationship management, onboarding, due diligence, compliance reviews, business introducer relationships, event participation, and requests relating to our products, services, or structures.

The website is intended for professional clients, eligible counterparties, prospective investors, business partners, introducers, and other professional contacts. The specific entity providing any services will be identified in the relevant contractual or onboarding documentation.

CONTACT

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your Personal Data is handled, you may contact us at: Email: legal@theblackcircle.com

WHAT IS PERSONAL DATA

"Personal Data" means any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual, including but not limited to:

  • name and contact details
  • identification information
  • financial or transactional information
  • online identifiers (e.g. IP address)

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We may collect and process the following categories of Personal Data:

Information you provide:

  • name, email, phone number
  • professional and financial background (where voluntarily provided)
  • identification documents (in onboarding contexts only)
  • any information submitted via forms or correspondence

Technical information:

  • IP address
  • browser type and version
  • device information
  • website usage data

Third-party information:

We may receive Personal Data from:

  • identity verification providers
  • compliance and screening providers
  • professional advisers or introducers

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We may use your Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • to respond to enquiries and provide information about The Black Circle Group, its affiliated entities, and its offerings;
  • to assess interest in our products, structures, and services;
  • to communicate with prospective professional clients, counterparties, business partners, introducers, and other professional contacts;
  • to support onboarding, due diligence, identity verification, KYC, AML, sanctions screening, source-of-funds checks, and related compliance processes where applicable;

LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process Personal Data only where we have a lawful basis, including:

DATA SHARING

We may share your Personal Data with:
